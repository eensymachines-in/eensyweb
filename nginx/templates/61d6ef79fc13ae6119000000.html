Versatile autolumin features

Versatality has been built right into Autolumin. Infact autolumin as though the name suggests, controls the illumination but thats only one cardinal use case where it can be used. Autolumin is a bunch of electrical switches that can be controlled from the internet. Its agnostic of what appliance is attached to it on the terminal. As long as the amperage ratings are respected Autolumin can be used in all cases when you need to schedule cloud connected switches to a clock. Nothing stops you from using autolumin on an aquarium.

Systematic aquariums

Aquarists, like me know well, this hobby is pure "labour of love". Precise control on the water body is about getting the Nitrogen cycle (1) right. Its an ultra fine balance and can only be achieved when you have a firm handle on the accessories working in synchrony.

  • Aeration
  • Light
  • Filtration
  • Heater & water temperature
  • pH of the water
**pH plays a vital role in the equilibrium of the tank. But we shall dedicate an entire blog in that effect

Remembering to turn on/off the accesories and operating them for the desired time is a full time job, no one can deny that. Slightest of slips in the schedule that lead to delta changes in the water and thus the health of the fish. The Nitrogen-cycle (1) of the tank is a delicate balance of conditions plus micro-bacter(7) there in. If you can get it right, you are an successful aquarist! Believe me only a few can get there. But, wait a minute, not if you have machines to help you do that job !! - Autolumin can be set to a clock and asked to turn switches at precise intervals.

Fish are creatures of habit

If you are starting new, somewhere a year down the lane you will realise that your pets are creatures of habit. Timely feeds, water changes, exact water temperature, sleep cycles are all factors in making them live much longer. I dont how underrated that statement is, but fact remains that fish need the environment in equilibrium and at max tolerate very gradual change. No natural water body changes its attributes suddenly, and hence the creatures dwelling in it have adapted to this phenomenon. Aquariums only mimic this environment and try to maintain similar conditions.


Autolumin to the rescue

The aquarium accesories that you were initially connecting to the electrical socket (direct ~AC) will now be re-routed via the Autolumin socket. For each of the switches that Autolumin provides we have made provisions for terminals to be connected. Once your accesories are rerouted via Autolumin, they come under the ambit of the managed clock from the cloud.
Next you get a handheld and make sure its online. Pull up your browser and thru the webapp Eensymachines provides you can securely alter the operating clock of all the switches.
