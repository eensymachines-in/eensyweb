The case of dying fish

Fish  are fun, and Im yet to come across a person who doesn't like them. Cat/Dog people wars will go on, but no one disagrees on having aquariums. Aquariums though are a posh possession, aren't really easy to maintain. If you have tried in the past- yes! Im talking about you. You ought to remember to turn the accessories ON/OFF and there is little to no tolerance on missing out to do that.

  • Lights stay on for a bit longer- you have algae bloom
  • Filter does not run 6 hours a day minimum- you have a smelly tank, ammonia spike
  • Aeration is below 5 hours fish suffocate
  • Heater does not run for a right time, fish get infections and tend to be lazy

Point is: aquariums are hinged on a fine balance of accesories' operation time. Autolumin again comes in handy. You can program your aquarium for operating hours from anywhere and enjoy your pets being pampered.
