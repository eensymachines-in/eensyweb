Imagine cloud enabled homes

Future homes would be the ones that understand & assist us, inmates a tad bit better. Why would you want to remember to turn on the lights, or check to see the aquarium temperature, turn on the garden sprinkler ? Wouldn’t it be cool if your home learns, memorizes and does all those chores ?

We re-imagined your homes with tiny-worker gadgets, so you can focus on the tasks that actually matter. After assembling the devices with their sensors we got them hooked onto the cloud so you can command these “workers” from anywhere, Yep, just about anywhere!

What we do

Programming single board computers is our forte. Our devices are programmed to be responsive and consume optimal resources. As a result you find our gadgets run surprisingly cooler! Keep an eye on the ventilator fan just in case you wont take our word for it.

We know the frustration of mobile app clutter. Eensymachines is high on mobile web. All you need to run the applications is a browser and an internet connection. Everything is just another web application, no downloads, no updates…

All our artefacts are cloud-native, They are all born to be online 24×7. For you, it means you can access your tiny machines from laptop, tablets, phones or practically any device that has a browser and an internet connection!

Why you'll love us

Each product has been handcrafted to precision. We take pride in wiring all the electronics by hand !! While most others would hide all the wiring drudgery beneath a black box, we take pride in showcasing our electronic parts underneath. Design is ultra-modular which means all the components are replaceable on the fly. Nevertheless in case of a malfunction we replace the the entire gadget. You get a shiny new piece, we reclaim the old one. That smile on your face is priceless!
